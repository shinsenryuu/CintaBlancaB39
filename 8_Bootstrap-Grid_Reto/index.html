Earth’s climate is now changing faster than at any point in the history of modern civilization, primarily as a result of human activities.

Bear Springs Blossom offers news on environmental issues. Read BSB conservation news online to explore the physical science of climate change and understand the impacts of climate change around the World.

The more you know about our climate the better are your decisions!
